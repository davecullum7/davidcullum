What makes me tick?

Imagine a pizza place owner having the ability to predict its sales volume on any given day simply by knowing what the barometric pressure and temperature is outside. Imagine the customer satisfaction when that business owner has the ability to have fresh ingredients delivered daily instead of having a weeks worth of ingredients lying around getting old and stale. Think about the cost savings in waste reduction and appropriate staffing.

The business world has seen a paradigm shift in how to make business decisions. If a business isn't collecting and analyzing its data to make better decisions quicker, they will be in trouble soon , if they aren't already struggling. My passion is finding what data needs to be collected, how to collect it, how to build and design systems to analyze that data quickly, presenting that vital information to executive teams providing them the opportunity to develop the knowledge needed to keep the business safe, stay ahead of the competition and grow revenue exponentially.
